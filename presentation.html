Signal Processing

  • 4th-order Butterworth bandpass (0.5–15 Hz)
  • Zero-phase filtering (filtfilt)
  • Gravity removal & magnitude computation
  • Adaptive peak detection for steps

Feature Engineering

  • 10 time-domain features × 4 channels
  • FFT spectral energy & entropy
  • Dominant & mean frequency
  • Sliding windows (2s, 50% overlap)

SIGNAL PROCESSING

From Noisy Data to Clean Signals

Raw accelerometer data is noisy and contains gravity. Our preprocessing pipeline:

% Butterworth bandpass filter [b, a] = butter(4, [0.5 15]/(Fs/2)); accelClean = filtfilt(b, a, rawAccel); % Orientation-independent magnitude mag = sqrt(ax.^2 + ay.^2 + az.^2); % Adaptive step detection [peaks, locs] = findpeaks(mag, ... 'MinPeakHeight', std(mag)*0.8, ... 'MinPeakDistance', round(Fs*0.35));

DEEP LEARNING

Bidirectional LSTM Activity Classifier

sequenceInputLayer(4) — ax, ay, az, magnitude
↓
bilstmLayer(128) — captures temporal patterns
↓
dropoutLayer(0.3) → bilstmLayer(64)
↓
fullyConnected(32) → softmax → classification

Why LSTM over Classic ML?

  • Captures temporal patterns in movement
  • Walking has a rhythm — LSTM learns it
  • Processes raw sequences directly
  • Bidirectional = reads forward AND backward
